Build your team budget
Each team has a cost based on strength. Pick 5 teams — your entry fee is the total of their costs, capped at $20. You can't load up on all the favourites!
2
Pick exactly 5 teams
Choose 5 national teams across any tiers, as long as the total doesn't exceed $20. You earn points every time your teams win or advance.
3
Multiple entries welcome
Want to enter more than once? Go for it — each entry is a separate fee. A unique team name is required for each entry so we can tell them apart on the leaderboard.
4
Earn points as the tournament progresses
Points are awarded for every group stage match your teams win (+3) or draw (+1), and for every knockout round they advance through. The further they go, the more points — with big bonuses for semi-finals, finals, and the champion.
